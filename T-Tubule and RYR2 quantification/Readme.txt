System requirements
The code has been tested on IDL (version 7.1) and Windows 10 Pro (version 20H2)

Installation guide
Install IDL as manufacturer indicates（https://www.l3harrisgeospatial.com/Software-Technology/IDL）

Instructions
1. Fill in the file name and its directory.
2. Compile the program and execute.
3. Left click 2 times in background region.
4.  Click one bright dot at lower left region and click another one at upper right region.
5. Keep left clicking until the image orientation is vertical and doule middle click.
6. Click lower left and then upper right to select the region for calculation.
7. Click the baselines of the first peak and complete.
